About

Hi, I'm Alan.

I'm a UK-based creative generalist, which is a tidy way of saying I've spent years bouncing (quite happily) between design, video/motion, web, and game projects.

I like work where there's a mix of craft and problem-solving. Sometimes that means tidying up something complicated until it becomes simple. Sometimes it means making something feel more human. And sometimes it means building something playful that still needs proper structure behind it.

I've worked with companies who need someone who can plug in fast, communicate clearly, and deliver. I'm comfortable collaborating with developers and non-designers, and I'm equally happy working solo when you just need the job done properly.

Outside of client work, I'm usually building game ideas with friends — the fun kind of projects where you get to obsess over tiny details that nobody asked for.
